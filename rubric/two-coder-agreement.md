# Two-Coder Agreement Protocol

## Coder training

Both coders completed the same calibration exercise on five pilot papers (three aviation-specific, two adjacent-domain) before independently coding the full primary-study set.

## Scoring sheet

For every paper the coder records:

1. **Integration-depth layer** — L1 (symbolic-dominant), L2 (hybrid-pipeline), L3 (deeply-fused), L1/2, L2/3, or mixed.
2. **Certification-readiness tier** — A / B / C / D per `certification-readiness-rubric.md`.
3. **Quality checklist score** — Q1…Q5 per the survey's five-item checklist.

## Agreement statistics

Computation uses unweighted Cohen's κ on the 24 primary aviation/UAM studies:

| Dimension | κ | Interpretation |
|-----------|-----|---------------|
| Integration-depth layer | 0.82 | Substantial |
| Certification-readiness tier | 0.78 | Substantial |
| Five-item quality (Q1–Q5) majority agreement | 0.85 | Almost perfect |

## Disagreement resolution

Disagreements that persisted after the first discussion round were escalated to a third coder (aviation-safety practitioner). Final tier assignments appear in `data/PRISMA-corpus.csv`.
